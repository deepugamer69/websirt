Major Achievements

Manchester United is the first English club to achieve the "Treble" (Premier League, FA Cup, and UEFA Champions League) in a single season (1998–99).

Competition Titles Most Recent
English League Titles (First Division/Premier League) 20 2012–13
FA Cup 13 2023–24
League Cup (EFL Cup) 6 2022–23
European Cup / UEFA Champions League 3 2007–08
UEFA Europa League 1 2016–17
FIFA Club World Cup 1 2008
FA Community Shield 21 2016

*Note: Manchester United also holds the record for the most top-flight league titles in English football history (20).
