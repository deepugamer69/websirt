Manchester United is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England. The club competes in the Premier League, the top division of the English football league system.It is one of the most decorated club in the history of football competing with global giants leaving an everlasting legacy on football.From Bankruptcy to Munich air disaster ,this club has survived countless obstacles and manages to remain as the best club in england.

Club History

Founded as Newton Heath LYR in 1878, the club changed its name to Manchester United in 1902. They are one of the most successful clubs in English football.

The Stadium

Their home ground, Old Trafford, has a capacity of over 74,000 and is the largest club football stadium in the United Kingdom.

Major Honours

  • 20 League Titles
  • 13 FA Cups
  • 3 UEFA Champions Leagues

Known as "The Red Devils," the club has a global fan base and a long-standing rivalry with Liverpool and Manchester City.
